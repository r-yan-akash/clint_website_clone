How Can I Help You?

Right now everyone has been affected by Covid. People are going through a multitude of emotions. The alternative tools I use are immediate to release the negative emotions such as anxiety and depression. It is not uncommon for a current day crisis to trigger past traumas. When dealing with the pandemic there can be many unexpected events that have taken place such as: loss of job or business, losing family members, children and school, abusive relationships, etc. Uncertain times could trigger past chilhood events when you were unsure of what was going to happen next. Childhood trauma is usually the root cause of many problems in our life. When you find the root cause of the problem the current day experience can change. I am a great detective at finding your negative beliefs and patterns. You will be surprised at what comes up in sessions that will help you to finally be able to understand where that problem or behavior is coming from so you can change it. Not by talking about it, but actually changing it in the unconsious part of the brain which really is the part that controls our life.

Working with the unconsious mind and body is imperative for lasting change. The mind body connection is everything when literally changing painful memories of trauma or abuse. Yes, you can change something that is so horrible that you think you cannot get over it. When we are done working together your painful memories will have no longer bother you.

Once you are able to release and let go of painful emotions; the mind will no longer look for ways to escape, causing pain in the body, creating depression, sadness, anger, or anxiety, or unwanted relationships, and behaviors.

About Laura

Laura is a Transformational Coach and Speaker, who has helped hundreds of people overcome their past and begin to live the life of their dreams. It is my core belief that we can overcome anything. When we change our patterns and beliefs that keep us stuck; there are truly infinite possibilities that come into our awareness. My training includes many healing modalities, techniques, and studies over the last 25 years. Some of the main techniques I use in meeting with clients will be FasterEFT/Eutaptics, EFT, Body Code, Trauma Training, and Addiction Protocols. I incorporate the rest of the holistic methods such as NLP, Hypnosis, and my TASA System, to help people release the hurt and pain of stressful emotions.
